# AIRA Scanner Roadmap

## Current state

AIRA Scanner is live as an early-stage research tool for detecting fail-soft patterns in modern software systems.

---

## Near-term priorities

- improve documentation and repo clarity
- expand detection rules
- improve output structure
- collect initial benchmark datasets

---

## Mid-term goals

- CI integration and strict mode
- rule classification (deterministic vs heuristic)
- reproducible scan comparisons

---

## Long-term direction

- multi-language support
- cross-repo comparison tooling
- research publication alignment

---

## Guiding principle

AIRA should evolve as a disciplined research tool first, and only become stricter or broader when its claims remain supportable.
